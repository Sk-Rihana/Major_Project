Downscaling of Satellite-Based Air Quality Map

About the Project

This project focuses on downscaling satellite-based air quality maps using advanced AI and machine learning techniques. Our goal is to provide high-resolution air quality data that can be used for better environmental monitoring and policy-making.

By leveraging satellite data, we aim to enhance the accuracy of air quality predictions, helping communities understand and respond to air quality issues more effectively.
